Hardware Assembly#

The Aeon system consists of a modular, scalable, and programmable habitat equipped with various interactive and non-interactive modules, such as foraging patches and nests. Here, you will find comprehensive guidelines and parts lists to help you replicate the main modules or inspire you to create your own.
