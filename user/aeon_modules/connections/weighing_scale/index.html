Weighing Scale Wiring Schematic#

In Aeon experiments, animals’ weights are monitored regularly with weight measurements taken whenever they enter the nest, which has a weighing scale attached to it.

Connections#

  1. The weighing scales are provided with power from a COM port connection from the PSU.

  2. A connection to the behaviour machine is made by wiring a connection through a RS232 port.
